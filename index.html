About

For 20 years I have been working as an instructional media developer, creating custom graphics, animations, interactive exercises, and instructional videos for higher education and large corporations.

I am an expert in most pieces of software in the Adobe Creative Suite package (Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.) and also work with Articulate Storyline 360 or Articulate Rise.

I can take your face-to-face course and help you turn it into a full fledged course, complete with learning objectives, interactive exercises, branching scenarios, voiceovers, modern accessibility compliance, custom animations, and more.
