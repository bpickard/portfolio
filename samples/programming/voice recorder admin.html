Voice Recorder FAQ

Q: My audio sounds choppy! How can I fix that?

A: Try increasing the gain on the microphone. If that doesn't work, try using a wired internet connection vs. a wireless connection.


Q: I can't hear any sound when I play back audio!

A: Please ensure you are using the proper microphone and that your speakers are turned on and working. Click the "change" button on the record tab and select a different microphone and try again.


Q: What's "Broadcast Mode"?

A:Broadcast mode will allow you to hear your own voice through your headphones when you speak, so you can get a better idea of what you sound like. Having this checked during recording is not recommneded as it could be distracting.
